 XBOX Mappings:

	A - Assist/Manual toggle 
		~ 1 click -> Assist
		~ 1 second hold -> Manual
	B - Holding pattern toggle
*	X - Throttle Lock
	Y - Emergency Recover


*	LB - Rudder left (5 degrees)
*	RB - Rudder right (5 degrees)
	LT - 
	RT - Throttle actuator

	UP    - PID Calibration toggle

	RS (X,Y) - (Aileron, Flaps/Spoilers)
	LS (X,Y) - (Rudder, Elevator)
	RS (click) - Holding pattern toggle
	LS (click) - Throttle Lock toggle


	Start  - Pair ATC & Plane
	Back   - Capture Runway
	Home   - Return home (light flashes)



Combinations:

	Start + Back  -  Flight toggle

	
	(While holding pattern active)
	A - Auto/Assist toggle
		~ 5 second hold -> Auto
		~ 1 second hold -> Assist
